# ⚖️ Moncho Data Scoring Standards

To maintain a "Top 1%" reputation, your data is evaluated against the official **Moncho Scoring System**. This 1-5 scale measures the qualitative value of an organization.

### 1. Innovation & Differentiation
- **Focus**: Uniqueness of business model, patents, R&D focus.
- **Goal**: Identify companies that aren't just copycats.

### 2. Market Traction & Growth
- **Focus**: Revenue growth, user base size, recent funding, market share.
- **Goal**: Identify companies with verified momentum.

### 3. Competitiveness
- **Focus**: Moat, barriers to entry, position vs incumbents.
- **Goal**: Identify companies that are hard to displace.

### 4. Product Depth
- **Focus**: Portfolio diversity, feature set maturity, UX/UI quality.
- **Goal**: Identify high-quality engineering and product design.

### 5. Social Proof & Impact
- **Focus**: Awards, sustainability, customer sentiment, verified status.
- **Goal**: Identify companies with external validation and positive impact.

---

### 🧠 The Analyst's Job
Your goal is to provide a **one-line rationale** for each dimension in your research notes. This helps the AI Judge and Human Reviewers verify your findings.

*Example Rationale (Innovation)*: "Pioneered a proprietary AI-routing algorithm that reduces delivery costs by 40% in emerging markets."
