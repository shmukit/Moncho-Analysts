## How analysts use this

| Role | Use the 10 sectors to |
|------|----------------------|
| **Data Ops** | Score orgs/products and draft facts **per sector**; Coverage Snapshot grades all 10 (🟢/🟡/🔴) |
| **GTM Ops** | Tie posts and newsletter issues to sector stories (RMG, agri, import HS chapters, etc.) |

**Discovery deliverable:** one row per sector in your Coverage Snapshot — landscape status, org/product count, biggest gap.

**Depth phasing (internal):** go deep where evidence supports it first; scaffold the rest with gap registers. All **10** remain in scope for the grant.
